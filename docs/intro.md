# Welcome to The A.P.O.L.L.O. Organization Docs

Welcome to the official documentation hub for **The A.P.O.L.L.O. Organization**! We are a collaborative group of friends and developers passionate about building, learning, and experimenting with code.

## About Us

The A.P.O.L.L.O. Organization is a shared space for projects, ideas, and open-source adventures. We are a close-knit team united by curiosity, creativity, and a love for technology. Our activities include:

- Collaborative software projects
- Learning and experimenting with new tech
- Sharing code, resources, and knowledge
- Supporting each other's growth as coders and creators

## Our Projects

Explore our current projects:

- **[A.P.O.L.L.O Discord Bot](./projects/apollo/intro)**: A feature-rich Discord bot with plugin architecture, utility and moderation commands, multi-instance scaling, and integrated subsystems including Nova.
- **[Nova Subsystem](./projects/nova)**: Na'vi word posting subsystem — originally a standalone bot, now integrated as a plugin inside Apollo v2.

## Getting Started

To contribute or learn more:

- Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/QgJcV7kP3a) for discussions and help.
- Check out our [GitHub Organization](https://github.com/The-A-P-O-L-L-O-Organization) for repositories.
- Follow our development standards and workflows.

## Guidelines

When working with our projects:

- Be respectful, kind, and patient
- Encourage learning and experimentation
- Promote collaboration and knowledge sharing
- Follow our development standards

Remember: "Together, we reach for the stars." Let's build something awesome!
